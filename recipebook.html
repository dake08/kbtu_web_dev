Recipe #1

Low Carb Cheese Crisps

  • Time: 10m
  • Ingredients: 3
Ingredients Quantity
shredded cheese 1 cup
chili powder 50 gramm
shredded cheese 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees

Step 2: Line a baking sheet (or two) with parchment paper.

Step 3: Using a measuring spoon, drop 1 tbsp piles of cheese about 2 inches apart (the cheese will spread so don't put them too close) on the baking sheet.

Recipe #2

Eggs

  • Time: 9m
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 12
water 1 cup

Step 1:Place a trivet at the bottom of the inner Instant Pot pan. Place eggs on top of trivet, stacking evenly if necessary.

Step 2: Add one cup of water to the pan. Close the lid and set Instant Pot for 5 minutes on Manual at HIGH pressure
